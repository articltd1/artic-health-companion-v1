# Admin Experience

## Vision
Define an administrative view that emphasizes system health, capacity, and patient care oversight while still feeling aligned with the ARTIC brand.

## Experience Goals
- Present high-level healthcare operations quickly.
- Highlight resource capacity and patient volumes.
- Surface AI-driven performance and quality metrics.
- Enable confident oversight without deep clinical detail.

## Key Screens
### Admin Dashboard
- System status summary: uptime, appointment volume, patient load
- Doctor coverage and capacity cards
- Alert panel for outstanding issues or compliance flags
- AI insights into care demand and predictive capacity

### Patient Summary
- Care cohorts and patient segments
- Highlights for high-risk populations and follow-up needs
- Resource utilization by service line

### Provider Management
- Doctor roster and availability
- Appointment capacity controls
- Team coordination and communication status

### Analytics & Reporting
- Usage and outcomes trends
- Adoption and engagement metrics
- AI performance indicators

## Trust & Credibility
- Use trusted data visualizations and polished KPI cards
- Include data provenance and update timestamps
- Provide clear action pathways for each metric

## User Needs
- Fast insight into platform utilization
- Confidence that operations are stable and clinically supported
- Clear indicators of risk or capacity constraints
- Easy navigation between patient, provider, and system levels

## Experience Improvements
- Add a compact admin summary panel on the authenticated shell
- Use KPI cards for capacity, demand, and performance
- Provide a system status strip for uptime and integration health
- Create a clear navigation path between operational dashboards and patient/provider detail
