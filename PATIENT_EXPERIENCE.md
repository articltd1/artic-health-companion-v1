# Patient Experience

## Vision
Create a patient experience that feels intelligent, supportive, and easy to navigate. Patients should perceive ARTIC as a trusted health companion rather than a generic health portal.

## Experience Goals
- Deliver immediate reassurance through branded trust signals.
- Focus the patient journey on actionable health outcomes.
- Make health intelligence feel clear and responsible.
- Create simple, empathetic interaction patterns.

## Key Screens
### Patient Dashboard
- Hero summary of current health status
- Upcoming appointments and care reminders
- AI recommendations and risk alerts
- Quick actions for booking care, predicting conditions, and reviewing records

### Disease Prediction
- Guide the patient through symptom capture with plain language
- Use visual signals for confidence and next best action
- Present results as insights, not raw diagnostics
- Provide clear guidance on when to seek care

### Appointments
- Surface status: pending, upcoming, completed
- Show timeline and required preparation
- Make cancellations/rescheduling easy and low friction

### Profile & Records
- Display personal information clearly
- Show care team and patient history summaries
- Make patient records accessible but not overwhelming

## Trust & Credibility
- Use health-focused blue / teal palette
- Add security and privacy cues
- Show AI as advisory and explainable
- Avoid clinical jargon in patient-facing copy

## User Needs
- Easy access to timely care
- Confidence that predictions are clinically grounded
- Fast path to doctor selection and appointment booking
- Clear explanation of results and next steps

## Experience Improvements
- Replace hero text with a concise value proposition
- Introduce a health summary card with metrics and action prompts
- Use soft data visualizations for risk and trends
- Convert appointment lists into identifiable care cards
- Provide a warm empty state when no data exists
