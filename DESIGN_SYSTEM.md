# ARTIC Design System

## Purpose
The ARTIC Design System provides a consistent visual and interaction language for the ARTIC Health Companion frontend. It ensures a polished, accessible, and medical-grade experience across patient, doctor, AI, and admin workflows.

## Brand Essence
- Professional
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Futuristic
- Clinical
- AI-powered
