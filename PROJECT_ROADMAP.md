# ARTIC Health Companion Project Roadmap

## Vision
Deliver an AI-powered digital healthcare ecosystem that improves access, quality, and operational efficiency for patients, clinicians, healthcare institutions, and public health stakeholders.

## Milestones
- **MVP Launch**: Authentication, patient and doctor management, appointment scheduling, health news, and AI chat assistant.
- **Clinical Foundation**: Electronic medical record support, patient history, secure data capture.
- **Telehealth Expansion**: Video consultation, messaging, and remote appointment coordination.
- **AI Health Companion**: Clinical decision support, intelligent health guidance, and patient triage.
- **Enterprise Integration**: Analytics, medication management, lab and pharmacy systems, hospital administration.
- **Future Mobility**: Mobile applications, IoT monitoring, and healthcare robotics.

## Quarterly Goals
- **Q1**: Stabilize core MVP workflows, validate authentication and appointment flows, document architecture.
- **Q2**: Add clinical record scaffolding and patient encounter capture.
- **Q3**: Enable telemedicine workflows and secure provider consultation features.
- **Q4**: Introduce AI companion capabilities and analytics pilot dashboards.

## Release Plan
- **Release 1.0**: Core patient and provider portal with booking and news.
- **Release 1.1**: Profile management and secure appointment workflows.
- **Release 2.0**: Electronic Medical Records integration.
- **Release 3.0**: Telemedicine and video consults.
- **Release 4.0**: AI-assisted clinical insights.
- **Release 5.0**: Healthcare analytics and reporting.

## Sprint Timeline
- **Sprint 1**: Authentication, role-based access, and user onboarding.
- **Sprint 2**: Appointment capture, doctor dashboard, and user appointment history.
- **Sprint 3**: Health content and chatbot integration.
- **Sprint 4**: Clinical record prototypes and secure data models.
- **Sprint 5**: Telehealth UI and session management.
- **Sprint 6**: AI workflow connectors and analytics experiments.

## Future Expansion
- **Electronic Medical Records**: structured patient records, clinical notes, and care plans.
- **Telemedicine**: secure video sessions, messaging, and provider scheduling.
- **AI Health Companion**: personalized health guidance, symptom triage, and predictive insights.
- **Health Analytics**: population dashboards, clinician KPIs, and operational metrics.
- **Medication Management**: prescriptions, reminders, and adherence tracking.
- **Laboratory Integration**: results ingestion and diagnostic workflows.
- **Pharmacy Integration**: order management and fulfillment.
- **Hospital Administration**: resource planning, occupancy, and staff scheduling.
- **Mobile Application**: patient and clinician mobile experience.
- **IoT Health Monitoring**: wearables, sensors, and remote monitoring.
- **Healthcare Robotics**: future robotics-assisted patient care and automation.
