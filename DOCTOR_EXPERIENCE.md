# Doctor Experience

## Vision
Design a clinical workspace that empowers doctors to manage care efficiently, review patient risk quickly, and trust the AI insights provided by the platform.

## Experience Goals
- Surface high-priority patient work clearly.
- Support fast triage and follow-up actions.
- Provide relevant clinical context without overload.
- Ensure the experience feels professional and credible.

## Key Screens
### Doctor Dashboard
- Overview of today’s schedule and activity
- Patient risk summary and urgent cases
- Metrics for appointment load and response time

### Appointment Work Queue
- Clear tabs for pending, upcoming, and completed
- Patient preview cards with key details
- Actions for review, accept, message, and mark complete

### Patient Record Access
- Structured medical record view
- Latest encounter summary and history
- AI-supported insights on risk factors and recommendations

### Profile Management
- Contact and specialization summary
- Availability or shifts if supported
- Secure access and logout

## Trust & Credibility
- Use a restrained professional palette with higher contrast
- Keep clinical language concise and precise
- Provide clear provenance for AI recommendations
- Show doctor-centric navigation and status

## User Needs
- Fast access to the day’s highest-priority work
- Confidence that the platform supports safe clinical decisions
- A clear view of patient history and appointment context
- Minimal clicks to perform routine tasks

## Experience Improvements
- Replace the simple tab list with a structured clinician workfeed
- Use progress chips for patient status and risk
- Add summary cards for patient volume and capacity
- Introduce a quick access panel for urgent alerts and follow-up tasks
- Standardize the doctor login/register look with the broader brand
